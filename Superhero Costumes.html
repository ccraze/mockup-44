Who wouldn't want to be a superhero, even if just for a day? Whether you are a Marvel or DC Comics fan, Costume Craze is the place to go for superhero costumes. We carry hundreds of superhero costumes for boys and girls (for all ages, including newborns) and men and women (including plus sizes). We even have superhero costumes for dogs. So whomever you are shopping for, Costume Craze has got you covered! Perhaps you'd like to be Superman, Spider-Man, Batman, the Hulk, Iron Man, or Thor? Or how about Supergirl, Batgirl, or Wonder Woman? We carry all of these superhero costumes and many, many more. We also carry hundreds of superhero costume accessories to complete your look--including makeup, wigs, gloves, belts, boots, weapons, and more! Partying or trick-or-treating as a group? Why don't you all go as members of the Justice League? And, of course, superheroes would be unemployed without their nemeses. So, yes, we have all of the favorite villains as well. The Joker, the Penguin, Poison Ivy, Catwoman, and more are ready to wreak havoc at your party, cosplay convention, or on Halloween night. Costume Craze can make all of this colossal superhero fun happen for you. We can also help with any questions before you order. So if you have any questions, a knowledgeable customer service superhero (they really are!) would love to assist you in any way.
